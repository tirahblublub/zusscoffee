There's no coffee without U

From the beginning, our dream was always to deliver specialty coffee as a daily necessity for ALL to enjoy. For many, specialty coffee is often seen as a luxury, something you treat yourself to on special occasions. We wanted to change this perception.

Every ZUS Coffee is a cup of clean, consistent and well-handled coffee. With the best quality ingredients, high-level coffee brewing technology and innovative business model, we are evolving the concept of coffee consumption to make specialty coffee affordable for everyone, everyday.

With over 39 million cups delivered to date, we only have our ZUSsies to thank for all our achievements thus far. Your amazing loyalty and support are what motivated us to keep our promise to you and ensure that ZUS Coffee is always a Necessity, not a Luxury.
